Campus life

Siater nivedita School

The philosophy that Sister Nivedita School abides by is to ensure an educational environment that is child-centered and the 'learning journey' of each and every child is a blissful one. Therefore in every parent-teacher gathering we endeavor to discover every child's distinctive qualities and nurture them to the maximum scope possible. Also, the academic studies are balanced with overall development so that the child can progress to the next class in the short run and blossom as a confident adult in the long run. Sister Nivedita School believes in the proven 'activity-based learning' model which is the central force behind CBSE's success.
The school was established in the year 1989 by Dr. Velchala Kondal Rao, an eminent educationist, linguist, poet and the former Director of the Telugu University. Sister Nivedita School is on the threshold of entering the three decade mark of its glorious service to social progress. Children, here, come to school not just to gain knowledge but to learn the essential threads of group dynamics, developing interpersonal skills and intra personal skills, moral and civil education. The philosophy that Sister Nivedita School abides by is to ensure an educational environment that increases the ability of students to focus and improve their cognitive abilities. The School believes in the proven activity based learning model which is the central force behind the success of CBSE and it has emerged as potential for understanding concepts easily and achieve goals of education.
